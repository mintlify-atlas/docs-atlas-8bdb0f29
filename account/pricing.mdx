BuzzTrip is currently in public beta and completely free to use. Every feature available today — unlimited maps, custom markers, collections, paths, labels, and collaborative sharing — is accessible to all users at no cost. Pro and Enterprise plans are being shaped for a future launch, and you can already subscribe to Pro to support development while the platform grows.

<Note>
  No credit card required. Sign up at [buzztrip.co](https://buzztrip.co) to get started for free.
</Note>

## What's Free Forever

The following capabilities are confirmed free on the Free plan and will remain so even after paid plans launch:

- **Unlimited maps** — create as many maps as you need with no cap
- **Unlimited markers** — drop as many points of interest as your adventures require
- **Unlimited collections** — organise markers into as many groups as you like
- **Paths** — draw and save routes and polylines on any map
- **Labels** — annotate your maps with custom text labels
- **Sharing** — share maps with collaborators using role-based permissions (owner, editor, viewer, commenter)

## Beta Note

During the beta period, all features are available for free to every user. The Pro subscription is currently available to purchase but has no additional features enabled beyond the Free plan — it exists purely as a way to support ongoing development.

<Info>
  The Pro plan displays a **"Supporting Development"** badge on the pricing page. Purchasing Pro during the beta period helps fund the engineering work that will bring advanced features — collaboration enhancements, import/export, mobile apps, and more — to all users faster.
</Info>
